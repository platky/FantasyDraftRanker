Draft Ranking Rules

The four non-playoff teams get initial priority. The percentages are as follows:

  • 10th - 40%
  • 9th - 30%
  • 8th - 20%
  • 7th - 10%

The first pick here gets to choose what draft position they desire. For example. First pick here wants 6th overall then second wants 9th overall and so on.

The six playoff teams do the same thing with the following percentages:

  • 6th - 40%
  • 5th - 30%
  • 4th - 15%
  • 3rd - 10%
  • 2nd - 4%
  • 1st - 1%
